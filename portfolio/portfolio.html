Unity

Brian Bui

Title: Unity

Medium: Photoshop

Size: 2276 x 1280px (900x540px in portfolio)

9/5/17

I based this piece off of an idea I had of unity where we can all succeed together or fail alone. In recent events people have begun to come together to help each other out in times of need which helped me come up with this idea. The silhouette of people are holding hands and soaring through the galaxy to reach an Earth that looks like two hands grasping to represent unity. They help each other soar to reach the Earth instead of letting anyone fall behind. My work would live online and would be projected on a screen.

    Net Art

  • Link to my Net Art Project.

    • Brian Bui

    Title: Net-Art

    Medium: github

    Keith and Mendi Obadike made a piece where they were selling Blackness. I wanted to recreate the piece using a different tone of skin. I thought of yellow because there are people who make fun of myself and fellow asians for our skin color. People would call asians as yellows or chinks all the time. I wanted to recreate an ebay page where you would be able to bid on the color of my skin and step into an asian's perspective. I wanted to sell my skin so that way the people saying all these harmful things will experience it when other people say the same things back to them. It's similar to what the Obadikes wanted to achieve in their piece.
