Artist Statement

My name is Brian Bui and I like to take pictures. The way I take pictures usually aren’t preplanned. I kind of just take pictures of whatever I find is appealing to me. My inspirations all depend on a variety of things whether I’ve seen it on instagram or if it was in the moment so I just take out my camera and take a picture of it. I do kind of preplan what I want out of the picture sometimes if it’s for someone else.

If one of my friends wanted me to take pictures for them then I would be kind of nervous about it so I would tend to pre plan where to go and what to do or how to do it. Most of the time when I’m out with my friends I’ll take pictures of whatever starts to catch my eye. Sometimes my car is parked in a nice spot or the background is really nice then I’ll just take a picture of it and mess around with it.
